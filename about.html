About Ready ER

Ready ER is a web service dedicated to streamlining communications between patients and hospitals, with an aim towards making health care more efficient while improving organization. The medical industry is essential, but has recieved little to no attention when it comes to improving communication. With Ready ER, lost medical records, ER overcrowding, wait times, and miscommunication will be a thing of the past.
